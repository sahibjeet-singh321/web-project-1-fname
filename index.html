What Is Drill Beat Music

Drill beat music is a type of hip-hop music that has a dark, hard, and aggressive sound.

It started in Chicago in the early 2010s and became popular in places like the UK and New York.

In the mid-2010s, the UK developed its own Drill scene, led by artists like 67, Headie One, and Digga D, with a more grime-influenced sound.

New York Drill, driven by artists like Pop Smoke and Fivio Foreign, later merged UK-style production with NYC street culture.

The name Dill beat comes from a music producer names Dillan Eli

About Dillan Eli

Dillan Eli is a well-known singer and songwriter.

He creates music that appeals especially to teens, with songs that are fun and easy to listen to. He shares his work online, where it has gained a lot of popularity.
